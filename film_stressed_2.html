Pulp Fiction

Starring: John Travolta, Samuel L. Jackson, Uma Thurman
Ratings: IMDB: 8.9/10, Rotten Tomatoes: 94%
Release date 1994

It is an American crime film written and directed by Quentin Tarantino, based on a story by Tarantino and Roger Avary. The film tells a few stories of criminal Los Angeles. The film's title refers to the pulp magazines and hardboiled crime novels popular during the mid-20th century, known for their graphic violence and punchy dialogue.
