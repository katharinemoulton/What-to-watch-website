Matilda

Starring: Mara Wilson, Danny DeVito
Ratings: IMDB: 6.9/10, Rotten Tomatoes: 90%
Release date: 1996

It is a 1996 American children's fantasy comedy film directed by Danny DeVito based on Roald Dahl's novel of the same name. The film is about a young genius named Matilda, who uses telekinesis to deal with her parents, who do not value education, and Agatha Trunchbull, the oppressive principal of Crunchem Hall Elementary School.
