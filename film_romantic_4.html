How I Met Your Mother

Starring: Josh Radnor, Neil Patrick Harris
Ratings: IMDB: 8.4/10, Rotten Tomatoes: 80%
First episode: 2005

It is an American sitcom that originally aired on CBS from September 19, 2005, to March 31, 2014. The series follows the main character, Ted Mosby, and his group of friends in New York City's Manhattan. As a framing device, Ted, in the year 2030, recounts to his son and daughter the events that led him to meet their mother.
