Chicken Run

Starring: Phil Daniels, Lynn Ferguson
Ratings: IMDB: 7/10, Rotten Tomatoes: 97%
Release date: 2000

Chicken Run is a 2000 British stop-motion animated comedy film produced. The plot centres on a band of chickens who see a smooth-talking Rhode Island Red named Rocky as their only hope to escape from certain death when the owners of their farm decide to move from selling eggs to selling chicken pot pies.
